Who We Are

PracticeGrid Solutions is a revenue cycle management company built to solve one critical problem in healthcare — revenue leakage.

Most billing companies focus on processing claims. We focus on optimizing the entire revenue cycle — identifying inefficiencies, preventing denials, and recovering lost revenue through structured workflows and revenue intelligence.

Our Mission

To help healthcare practices maximize revenue by eliminating inefficiencies and reducing preventable losses.
