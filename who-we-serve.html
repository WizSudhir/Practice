Medical Specialties We Support

Every medical specialty has unique billing rules, payer requirements, and clinical workflows. PracticeGrid delivers specialty-focused revenue cycle management designed to support providers across a wide spectrum of healthcare disciplines.

Podiatry

Routine foot care, wound treatment, and surgical podiatry procedures with complex Medicare billing rules.

Behavioral Health

Mental health and psychiatric billing including therapy sessions, telehealth services, and payer authorization workflows.

Internal Medicine

Comprehensive billing support for chronic disease management, preventive care, and complex E/M coding.

Durable Medical Equipment (DME)

Specialized billing for medical equipment providers including compliance, documentation, and payer rules.

Pediatrics

Preventive visits, immunizations, and pediatric care billing across commercial and Medicaid payers.

Cardiology

Advanced cardiovascular billing including diagnostic testing, procedures, and complex modifier requirements.
